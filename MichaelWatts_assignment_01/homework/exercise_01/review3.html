03: BBC Storyworks - Age of Change

www.bbc.com/storyworks/specials/age-of-change

DESIGN

I appreciate that the main scrolling page isn't filled with text but a simple amount to get you started. Color choices for each subject made sense in creating their own universe, but it takes away from the overall cohesion of the website.

USABILITY

My grandmother could definitely navigate through each page while being enchanted. I like that the return to home button is directly in the middle top of the page where your eyes look... grandmother approved! It’s simple and effective.

CONTENT

It’s very clear what the subject is, and the site guides us well through the subject matter, while not bombarding us. There are moments for reading and moments for watching video.

CREATIVITY

They use just the right amount of parallax scrolling, while adding a small touch of animated objects, like a hummingbird, quite realistic and adding to the dimensionality. I especially like that the lines of descriptive text under the thumbnail images spread vertically a little when you hover over them.
