01: Jacky Winter's dedicated home for animation

flutter.productions

DESIGN

Animated text works well with the matching color palette in the page tabs. I also like that each page has its own color that tints the images until you hover over them to reveal the real colors.

USABILITY

My grandmother would get very lost as the landing page is a little overwhelming with a lot of movement. Although the animation is cute, it took me a while to be able to focus and figure out exactly what the site was. I had to read “Jack Winter’s home dedicated to animation” a few times to understand.

CONTENT

They have a lot of content and show a large amount of portfolio work, even too much. Perhaps a smaller amount could effectively represent the aesthetic and skill.

CREATIVITY

There is an attempt to be creative in how the layout is very different with each tab, revealing a new page with a different width, but I don’t buy it. All of this compresses the mass amount of information and images, making the site feel overwhelming and claustrophobic.
